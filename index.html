About Me

I'm Yixuan Chen, currently an International Visiting Graduate Student at the University of Toronto, supervised by Prof. Baochun Li. I earned an MSc in Machine Learning from MBZUAI, supervised by Prof. Samuel Horvath and a BSc in Applied Mathematics from XJTLU with First Class Honours.

My research focuses on distributed and federated learning, and machine learning. I aim to bridge the gap between machine intelligence and physical systems.

Enhancing Federated Learning Through Low‑Rank Adaptation and Singular Value Decomposition

Yixuan Chen

This project proposes SVD‑LoRA, a method that enhances federated learning by combining low-rank adaptation with truncated SVD, effectively improving performance on non-IID data while reducing communication costs by up to 60% without sacrificing accuracy.
